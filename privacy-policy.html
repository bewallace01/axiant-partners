Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Last Updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

Axiant Partners ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our business loan matching service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Business information (company name, industry, years in business)
  • Financial information (loan amount requested, annual revenue, credit score range)
  • Any other information you choose to provide in your application

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various business and commercial purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Matching your business with appropriate lending institutions and financial service providers
  • Communicating with you about your loan application, our services, and third-party offers that may be of interest
  • Providing customer support and responding to your inquiries
  • Improving our services, developing new features, and conducting business analytics
  • Creating user profiles, conducting market research, and developing commercial insights
  • Generating revenue through data licensing, sharing arrangements, and commercial partnerships
  • Marketing and advertising our services and those of our partners, affiliates, and business associates
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights and business interests

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share, transfer, license, or otherwise disclose your information to third parties for various business and commercial purposes, including but not limited to:

  • Lending Partners and Financial Service Providers: We share your application information with financial institutions, lending partners, credit providers, and other financial service entities that we believe may be a good match for your business loan needs or may offer related financial products and services.
  • Business Partners and Affiliates: We may share your information with our business partners, affiliates, subsidiaries, and other entities with whom we have business relationships for purposes including marketing, lead generation, business development, and the provision of complementary services.
  • Data Brokers and Marketing Partners: We may share, license, or sell your information to third-party data brokers, marketing companies, advertising networks, and other commercial entities for purposes including targeted advertising, market research, analytics, and lead generation.
  • Service Providers and Vendors: We may share information with third-party service providers, vendors, contractors, and agents who perform services on our behalf, including but not limited to data processing, analytics, customer service, payment processing, and technology services.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction to the acquiring entity or successor organization.
  • Commercial and Marketing Purposes: We may use and share your information for our own commercial purposes, including but not limited to developing and marketing products and services, conducting market research, creating user profiles, and generating revenue through data licensing and sharing arrangements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, court order, or in response to valid requests by public authorities, regulatory bodies, or law enforcement agencies.

By using our services, you acknowledge and consent to the sharing, licensing, and potential sale of your information as described herein. We do not provide you with the ability to opt-out of all information sharing, and your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of these practices.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
